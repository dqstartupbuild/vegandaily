# Possible Features (Not Yet Implemented)

Based on a scan of the codebase, README, and docs, these common recipe-app capabilities are not currently present. Each item is a brief description of what would be added.

## Cooking & Execution
- **Step-by-step cooking mode**: Focused, full-screen steps with swipe/next/prev and large text for hands-free use.
- **Timers tied to steps**: Inline timers for specific instruction steps with alerts and background support.
- **Keep-awake mode**: Prevent the screen from sleeping while cooking a recipe.
- **Ingredient checklist**: Tap to check off ingredients as you prep.

## Planning & Organization
- **Meal planning calendar**: Assign recipes to dates/meals across a week and revisit the plan.
- **Shopping list builder**: Add ingredients from selected recipes and merge quantities by unit.
- **Recipe collections**: Create custom lists (e.g., “Quick Weeknights”) beyond bookmarks.
- **Recently viewed**: Quick access to last-seen recipes.

## Discovery & Filtering
- **Advanced filters**: Filter by tags, difficulty, prep time, nutrition, or ingredients.
- **Sorting**: Sort by time, difficulty, calories, or popularity.
- **Dietary/allergen filters**: Exclude ingredients like nuts, soy, gluten, etc.
- **Search suggestions**: Autocomplete or recent searches.

## Nutrition & Scaling
- **Scaled nutrition**: Recalculate nutrition values when servings change.
- **Macro targets**: Filter or recommend based on calorie/protein goals.
- **Unit conversion**: Convert between metric/imperial units.

## Social & Community
- **Ratings**: 1–5 star ratings per recipe.
- **Comments**: User feedback per recipe.
- **User submissions**: Upload or propose new recipes.

## Sharing & Export
- **Share recipe**: OS-level share sheet or copy link/text.
- **Print/export**: Simple print view or PDF export.

## Accounts & Sync
- **User accounts**: Optional login for syncing data.
- **Cross-device sync**: Sync bookmarks, plans, and preferences via backend.

## Monetization
- **Paywall / feature gating**: Gate Browse/Bookmarks or premium features (not implemented).
- **Subscriptions**: RevenueCat integration for payments.

## System & UX
- **Dark mode**: Theme toggle or system-driven theme.
- **Push notifications**: Remind users of daily recipes or meal plans.
- **Image optimization**: Cached/optimized loading for remote images if added later.
